Buse Yılmaz

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Dr. Didem Unat's group ParcoreLab, Koc University, Turkey. I obtained my PhD from Department of Computer Science, Ozyegin University, Turkey under the supervision of Dr. Baris Aktemur. My research interests include high performance computing, program generation and autotuning. I enjoy building tools and libraries doing program analysis and profiling taking advantage of compilers such as LLVM and machine learning. Currently, I'm working on sparse solvers.

Previous Affiliation

Before joining ParcoreLab, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Virginia Tech (2016-2018).

I was an instructor at Department of Computer Science, Ozyegin Unversity, Turkey (2016).
